Chicken Biryani

A flavorful and aromatic rice dish made by layering spiced chicken with fragrant basmati rice, caramelized onions, and a blend of rich spices like cardamom, cloves, and turmeric. Slow-cooked to perfection, this dish is often served with raita or kachumbari, making it a popular choice for festive occasions and special meals.

Kuku Paka

A delicious Swahili dish of grilled or stewed chicken cooked in a rich and creamy coconut milk sauce. Flavored with turmeric, garlic, ginger, and chili, it offers a perfect balance of spice and creaminess. Commonly served with rice, chapati, or naan, it's a coastal favorite known for its bold and comforting flavors.

Pilau

A fragrant and spiced rice dish popular in Swahili cuisine. Cooked with aromatic spices like cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, and cumin, it is often paired with meat, chicken, or vegetables. Known for its rich flavors and inviting aroma, pilau is a favorite for celebrations and communal meals.

Samaki wa Nazi

A flavorful Swahili dish featuring fish cooked in a creamy coconut milk sauce. Enhanced with spices like turmeric, garlic, and ginger, it is often served with rice, chapati, or ugali. This coastal delicacy is celebrated for its rich, aromatic, and comforting taste.
